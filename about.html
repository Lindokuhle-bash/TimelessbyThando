Meet Thando Ngwenya

Thando Ngwenya is the creative force behind Timeless by Thando, a fashion label rooted in elegance, self-expression, and classic design. She recently graduated from the Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT), where her passion for fashion was refined into a powerful artistic voice.

Our Story

In 2025, just after completing her studies at CPUT, Thando launched her debut brand, Timeless by Thando, with a vision: to create fashion that transcends the moment and lives on through generations. Her work blends traditional elegance with a bold, modern edge—crafted to empower and inspire.

Design Philosophy

  • Enduring Aesthetics — Timeless forms reinterpreted through a contemporary lens.
  • Conscious Craft — Thoughtful design, ethical materials, and sustainability in focus.
  • Empowerment Through Style — Each piece is designed to make the wearer feel powerful and unique.
